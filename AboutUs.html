About Insights

‘Insights’ is a podcast brand of ‘Self-help and Motivation ‘service that is provide connection between Professional Speakers and their Listners. This application Provides an easy-to-use abstraction over the sea of podcasting shows in the field of motivation and encouragement. This application is designed to make it very easy for the listener to search for motivational podcast speaker’s video Track or Audio Track. There are two benefits of ‘Insights’ application. First, it stores all of a user’s listening history and this wide-spanning amount of data allows us the opportunity to generate playlists that takes a user’s Interest into account. Second, it has an expansive bank of Audio/video for ‘self-help & Motivation’ service available so there is no worry of not having enough Podcast track for the user.
